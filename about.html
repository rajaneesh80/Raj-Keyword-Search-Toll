About Us

What We Do

ISKA London is the most extensive database of searches from real Amazon shoppers. Since 2012, we’ve collected over 1.6 billion keywords and helped nearly 100,000 entrepreneurs grow their businesses.

We collect searches from the Amazon search bar; this allows us to see into the minds of shoppers – your customers – and show you how they’re searching. With the power of our keyword data, you can discover new product opportunities, increase traffic to your product listings, improve your PPC campaigns, increase sales, and grow your business.

Amazon is Evolving – and So Are We

You might know us as the world's largest and most powerful database of global Amazon keywords. And, you wouldn’t be wrong, but we’re also much more than that now.

We offer a suite of tools specifically designed to achieve one goal: to help you grow a sustainable business.

It all starts with our proprietary algorithms created by our data scientists for keyword acquisition via the Amazon search bar.

Keyword Tool

Once all that data is collected, we comb through it, analyze it, and present it to you in a format that’s clear and easy to understand in our Keyword Tool. But this isn’t the same tool from 2012. It’s been improved to include keyword search history and trends, page one ASIN results and metrics collected in real-time, reverse-ASIN search, related keyword suggestions, and more.

There are endless uses for accurate keyword data, so if you need help finding a good place to start, check out our MW Guide on the left navigation bar.
