Frequently Asked Questions

What is Iska London

Iska London compiles and monitors keyword searches and search volume data from buyers worldwide. This allows us to derive unique market insights; and help sellers boost their product visibility and discover new opportunities.

Our goal is simple: To help online sellers, like you, choose the best keywords for your product listings and ppc ad campaigns so you can reach more customers.

How frequently is data updated?

Keyword data on Iska London is updated at least once a month. Though we are always collecting data, we do not publish new data on our site until we are confident in the accuracy of the information.
